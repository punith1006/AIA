8. Risk Assessment

Primary Risks

  • IP/Product Ownership Ambiguity: Public research did not confirm that Global Knowledge Technologies owns or operates the "Master AI-FlashCards" Leitner-based product; this ambiguity represents legal and GTM risk if product claims are made without verification.
  • Limited Financial Transparency: Lack of public financial statements and conflicting employee counts complicates evaluation of buying power and delivery capacity.
  • Competitive & Market Risk: Highly fragmented training market and digital EdTech platforms create price and feature pressure.
  • Operational Scalability: If heavy reliance exists on in-person or physical delivery, scaling to large enterprise deployments could require additional investment.

Mitigations

  • Confirm IP ownership and licensing via direct inquiry or contractual review before co-marketing or claims about product ownership.
  • Use pilot/POCs to test capacity and ensure supply-chain resilience for any physical training materials.
  • Negotiate phased payment and delivery terms tied to performance milestones to reduce financial exposure. (Analyst recommendation — internal)
